Enhancing Education with Technology

Pedagogical Use of Technology

The Pedagogical Use of Technology

This module is envisioned as 10 hours of synchronous and 10 hours asynchronous learning. The module would reflect the latest developments in the field of pedagogical use of technology such as gamification, artificial intelligence, immersive learning with VR and AR and video assisted learning, evidence-based approaches to training, particularly in an online environment and with adults as learners and incorporate the ICE framework.

Proposed Date: November 1, 2022- November 25, 2022
Mode: Online
Number of hours :10
Number of participants: Open
Participant Profile: K-3 Teachers

Digital Leadership and Change Management

Digital Leadership and Change Management

This module will reflect the latest developments in the field of pedagogical use of technology such as gamification, artificial intelligence, immersive learning with VR and AR and video assisted learning, evidence-based approaches to training, particularly in an online environment and with adults as learners and incorporate the ICE framework.

Proposed Date: February 1-25
Focus: To strengthen education leaders' capacity to support the transition to digital learning modalities
Mode: Online
Number of participants: 100
Number of hours :10
Participant Profile: Principals, National Focal Points, Education Officers, Curriculum Officers
Number of hours :10

Quality Assurance and Review of Learning Content

Quality Assurance and Review of Learning Content on iLearn Platform

This module is aimed at developing a cadre of competent learning content reviewers and evaluators in the OECS. Participants will be expected to gain full competency in applying the OECS Instructional Content and Engagement (ICE) Framework as a standard for selecting, creating and evaluating learning content in the OECS

Proposed Date: November 1, 2022- November 25, 2022
Mode: Online
Number of hours :10
Number of participants:15 per Member State
Participant Profile: High performing Content Developers and Reviewers on the iLearn platform as well as educators who have participated in Effective Pedagogies in Distributed Teaching and Learning (EPDTL) with the Eastern Caribbean Joint Board of Teacher Education (ECJBTE).

Leadership for Adoption and Mainstreaming of OER

Leadership for Adoption and Mainstreaming of OER

This module is for education leaders and it is aimed at strengthening their abilities to lead the integration of OERs at their schools and districts, as well as leading their own OER development activities.

Proposed Date: October 10th, 2022 - November 10th, 2022
Mode: Online and Face-to-Face (St. Lucia)
Number of hours :15
Number of participants: 2 per Member State
Participant Profile: DECI National Focal Points and any other officer in a related field who can facilitate future training.

Augmented Reality (AR) Training

Augmented Reality (AR) Training

This module is intended to prepare teachers for implementing simple Augmented Reality interventions in their classrooms. Teachers will learn how to design, select and create text, audio, graphic and video content to augment resources such as textbooks using a suitable Augmented Reality platform.

Focus: The development of AR resources to complement traditional resources
Mode: Face-to Face
Number of participants: 20 participants per member state
Number of hours :20
Participant Profile: Primary school teachers grades K-6

Digital Content Development

Digital Content Development

This module is for teachers and educators who want to learn how to use authoring tools to create and remix digital learning content. This training will focus on content development on the OECS iLearn platform.

Focus: Using content authoring tools on iLearn platform to upload, create and remix digital learning resources
Mode: Online
Number of participants: (TBD) per Member State
Number of hours :10
Participant Profile:Primary School Teachers (K-6)
